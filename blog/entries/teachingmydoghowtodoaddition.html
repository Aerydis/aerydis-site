Teaching My Dog How to Do Addition

Home About Blog Projects

I have been teaching my dog how to do addition. It is a work in progress, but I am making good progress. I started by teaching him to recognize numbers. I would show him a card with a number on it and say the number out loud. He would then bark the number back to me. It was a bit of a challenge at first, but he eventually got the hang of it.
Next, I taught him to recognize the plus sign. I would show him a card with a plus sign on it and say "plus". He would then bark "plus" back to me. Again, it was a bit of a challenge, but he eventually got it.
Now that he can recognize numbers and the plus sign, I am working on teaching him how to add two numbers together. I will show him two cards with numbers on them and say "what is this plus this?" He will then bark the answer back to me. It is still early days, but I am confident that he will get it eventually.
Teaching my dog how to do addition has been a fun and rewarding experience. It has also been a great way to bond with my dog and spend quality time together. I am looking forward to seeing how far we can go with this project!
